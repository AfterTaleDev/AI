ENTRY LOG: PROJECT C-1
SUBJECT: PROJECT CORE
AUTHOR: W.D. GASTER


The blueprints are complete. After months of theoretical mapping, the foundation of the System is finally manifesting in physical space. It is... larger than I anticipated. The scale is necessary, of course; to harness the very soul of the Underground, one cannot be modest.

The heat from the magma below is already fluctuating in rhythm with the initial thermal conduits. It feels like a heartbeat. A bit more than minimal activity—just enough to know the Core is 'awake'—but not yet at a level that threatens the stability of the surrounding cavern.

I have briefed the team. We are no longer just dreaming. We are building the future, piece by heavy piece. Darker, yet darker, the shadows of the machinery grow.

I will monitor the output levels closely. We cannot afford a surge this early."
